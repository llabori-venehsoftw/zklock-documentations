The Problem We Solve

Current smart lock solutions face significant challenges in the condominium market:

  • Privacy Concerns: All existing solutions collect and store user identity data
  • Centralization Risk: Cloud dependency creates single points of failure
  • High Costs: $200-$500 per unit with $5-15/month subscription fees
  • Vendor Lock-in: Proprietary ecosystems with expensive switching costs
  • Limited Integration: Poor compatibility with building-wide management systems

Our Solution

ZKLock introduces the world's first zero-knowledge proof-based smart building access control system, built on Cardano's Midnight L2 blockchain:

Privacy-First Architecture

Zero-knowledge proofs ensure access privacy while maintaining comprehensive audit trails. Users can prove their authorization without revealing their identity.

QR Code Innovation

Dynamic, time-bound QR codes with AES-256 encryption provide secure, universal access that works offline and across different lock brands.

Blockchain Integration

Built on Cardano Midnight L2 for programmable privacy, regulatory compliance, and seamless DeFi integration for building communities.

Building Management

Comprehensive dashboard for property managers to control access across thousands of locks, manage residents, and generate detailed reports.

Hardware Flexibility

Retrofit compatibility with existing doors and integration with current property management systems through open APIs.

Emergency Systems

Secure override mechanisms, physical key backup, and 24/7 emergency access with complete audit logging.
